Getting started is simple! Create an account, upload details and images of your surplus food, and set a discounted price. Our platform connects you with local customers ready to purchase surplus items, reducing waste and boosting your revenue.

We welcome a variety of businesses, including grocery stores, bakeries, restaurants, cafés, and wholesalers. If your business generates surplus food, Savour can help you find customers to reduce waste and increase profits.

Savour promotes your surplus items to local users actively searching for deals. By joining, you increase foot traffic, attract new customers, and enhance your brand’s sustainability image – all while minimizing waste.

Listing surplus items is free, and we only take a small commission when your items sell. This means there’s no risk in getting started, and you only pay when you benefit from the sale.

We encourage businesses to list items that are safe and high-quality but nearing their best-before dates. Clear labeling and transparency ensure customers know what they are purchasing, fostering trust and repeat sales.
